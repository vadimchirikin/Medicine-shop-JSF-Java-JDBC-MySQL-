Citrus belongs to the genus, where a large number of interspecific and intraspecific hybrids of the family Rutaceae. The main areas of diversity and ancient culture of citrus fruits are India, China and South East Asia, hence the first citrus fruits were introduced to the West and Southwest, to the countries of the Mediterranean region. Evergreen low, 3-6 (12) m tall, the trees, sometimes shrubs. The leaves are ovate or lanceolate, thick, almost leathery, with a translucent internal glands, which contain essential oils with a strong odor, and in the axils of thorns. Petiole winged. The flowers are white or pink, with a pleasant aroma, solitary or in groups of several. Many citrus fruits are valuable industrial crops (lemon, orange, mandarin, grapefruit and some others), they are widely bred in many tropical and subtropical countries, as well as in countries in the subtropical regions. Citrus fruits are in the collections of botanical gardens, cultivated in a number of gardening under glass and distributed as houseplants. Some of the citruses, commonly known as fruit, while interesting as an evergreen ornamental plants. In the juices of different citrus fruits contain a lot of valuable biologically active substances, other than those listed above. These substances contribute to the strengthening of human health. Thus, clinical trials have shown that one of them - hesperidin - has a vessel strengthening effect. In the preserves and jams from the mandarins in the high concentration of stored vitamin A, P and C, mineral salts, sugars, organic acids, characteristic of fresh fruit..
