Lime - an evergreen plant of the genus Citrus, belonging to the family Rutaceae. It is a small tree (1.5 to 5 m) with dense foliage, branches of which cover the short spines. Lime fruits like lemon, egg-shaped, green or yellow. Homeland of the plant - the Malay Peninsula, located in Southeast Asia. On the territory of the Mediterranean lime appeared in the second half I thousand BC Lime trees are grown on a commercial scale began in the Antilles for the first time - in the 70s of the XIX century were engaged in cultivation of these plants on the island of Montserrat. Lime grows in almost all tropical countries, especially citrus, this popular, where due to high humidity do not grow lemons - as these plants tolerate these conditions. At temperatures below zero lime trees do not survive. Most of the fruits of lime on the international market for exports, Mexico, India, Cuba and Egypt. Lime belongs to the oldest members of the genus Citrus. It is well known to us a lemon - nothing like a hybrid of lime and other citrus - etrog. The fruit of the plant differs from the more thin-skinned lemons, and the color of the skin - usually it's green (and therefore the fruit is sometimes called lime green lemon.) Or yellowish-green, sometimes yellow. Flesh greenish, very juicy and tart. There are many varieties of lime, which is usually divided into two main groups: sour and sweet. Lime is used in cooking as well as other citrus fruits. Lime juice acidic than lemon zest and flavor - more saturated and vivid. Lime eat fresh, adding to the variety of drinks and dishes to make them acid and flavor. Lime makes soft drinks such as lemonade (limead). Dried fruit drink manufacturers use «Sprite». Lime - a necessary component of many alcoholic drinks like gin and tonic, mojito, margarita. The pulp and peel confectioners used - in many popular cake Lime and lime pie. In the Mexican and Latin American cuisines lime - one of the most important ingredients in the sauce recipes spicy dishes of meat and fish. Regular consumption of this fruit prevents the development of caries, strengthens the gums and reduces their bleeding. Besides lime - a great energizer, and it must necessarily add to the diet in the winter to improve the tone and the prevention of seasonal depression.
